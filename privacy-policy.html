Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 360° Builds ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or submit your information through our property listing pages.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address (if provided)
  • The property you expressed interest in

We may also collect non-personal information such as browser type, device type, and pages visited through standard analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you text message updates about the property you expressed interest in
  • Notify you of build progress milestones
  • Connect you with the property's builder
  • Send you information about other available properties (only if you have opted in)
  • Improve our website and services

3. Text Message Communications

By submitting your phone number on any 360° Builds property page, you consent to receive text messages from 360° Builds regarding the property you expressed interest in. These messages may include:

  • Build progress updates
  • Virtual tour links
  • Builder contact information
  • Property availability updates

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information with:

  • The property builder you expressed interest in, for the purpose of following up on your inquiry
  • Service providers who assist us in operating our website and sending communications (such as Twilio for text messaging and Formspree for form processing)

These service providers are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only to provide services to us.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or until you request deletion. You may request removal of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP
  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions or concerns

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
